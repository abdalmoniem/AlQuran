<h1>The Holy Quran</h1>
<p>
  The Holy Quran is an app that allows you to listen to chapters of the Quran, voiced
  different reciters. It also supports Android Auto.
</p>
<h1>Features</h1>
<ul>
  <li>Listen to chapters of the Quran, voiced by different reciters.</li>
  <li>Support for Android Auto.</li>
  <li>Listening without an internet connection</li>
  <li>Pause and play.</li>
  <li>Seek to a specific position in the surah.</li>
  <li>Choose your preferred reciter.</li>
</ul>
